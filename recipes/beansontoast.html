Beans on toast

Description

Beans on toast are my absolute favourite. They may seem simple at first glance, but it takes real skill to craft a true classic.


Ingredients

  • 1 Can of beans
  • 2 Slices of bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat up the can of beans.
  2. Toast the bread.
  3. Butter the bread and pour on the beans.
